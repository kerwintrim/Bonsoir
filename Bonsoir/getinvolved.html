Donations

A simple way one can help out is through donations. Any monetary or canned food item will go a long way in assisting these children.

Fundraising

To help jump start our initiative and keep the gears turning, Bonsoir will have fundraisers perodically. These may be in the form of selling goods or food to raise money for the orphans in these remote villages.

Spreading Awareness

As a fellow member of the movement, you can spread awareness about the case in many ways. One common way is through sharing our website to your loved ones. Another way is using social media to share our website and inform people about our cause.
