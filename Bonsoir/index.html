Changing Haiti through cultivation

Bonsoir Haiti is an organization geared towards granting resources to orphans of Haiti so that they may be equipped to improve their lives and change the Dynamic of Haiti. These orphans are located in remote villages that lack a steady flow of resources. Bonsoir aims to supply these children with resources but more importantly, teach them how to produce their own resources.
